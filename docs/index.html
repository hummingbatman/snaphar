How it works

01 / Start

Hit record

SnapHAR attaches the DevTools protocol to your tab behind the scenes.

02 / Reproduce

Trigger the bug

Reload and click around. Nothing is cleared on navigation.

03 / Export

Save the .har

A valid HAR lands in your Downloads, ready to share or re-import.
